Why It Matters

Plastic waste in oceans and rivers harms marine life and ecosystems. We are working to raise awareness and take action to protect our water sources.

Our Goals

  • Promote plastic recycling and alternatives
  • Organize clean-up campaigns
  • Educate communities about the impact of plastic waste

How You Can Help

  • Use reusable bags and bottles
  • Volunteer in local clean-up drives
  • Educate others about the impact of plastic
  • Support policies that reduce plastic use
